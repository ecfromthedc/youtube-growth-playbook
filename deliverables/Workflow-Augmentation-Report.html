The Moat — what makes us the best.

Tools alone don't make us the best. Everyone can buy Veo 3.1 or Nano Banana 2. The moat is the integration — the proprietary workflows + accumulated channel data + in-house Claude skills that no competitor replicates without a year of dev work. Here are the five edges we build.

EDGE 01

Proprietary Prompt Library & Skills

Every prompt, skill, and SOP in our system gets versioned, refined, and accumulated. After 6 months of channel ops, we have prompts no other agency has — tested against thousands of videos. That's the compounding asset.

EDGE 02

Multi-Channel Pattern Library

Running multiple channels in parallel means we accumulate signal faster than solo operators. What works in aviation gets tested in finance. Pattern recognition across niches becomes our compounding edge.

EDGE 03

End-to-End Local Pipeline

Clipify (local), VibeVoice/RVC (local), Nano Banana MCP (local agent + cloud API), Claude Code orchestration. We're not renting SaaS workflows — we own them. Margin advantage at scale.

EDGE 04

Verification & Quality Gates

Scott's "AI is a tool, not a replacement" principle codified into actual checklists: Google Trends gate, Marc's hooks scoring, CTR/APV diagnostic matrix. Every video clears the gates before publishing. Quality is non-negotiable.

EDGE 05

Speed Without Sacrificing Quality

AI-augmented stack runs at 3–5x the velocity of pure-human teams while maintaining ship-ready quality. Two videos per week per channel becomes structurally easy, not a heroic push. That's how we credibly run 10+ channels at once.
