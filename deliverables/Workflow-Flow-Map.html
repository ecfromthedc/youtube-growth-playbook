The team's job in this pipeline

Own the niche strategy · run the 3 critical gates (Niche Verified, Strategist Approval at Topic + Script, Pre-Upload Checklist) · interpret analytics · decide what to double down on.
The AI does the grinding. Specialists handle the artistry — final scripts, thumbnail polish, editor finesse — where AI can't quite replicate yet.
