# Executive Summary — YouTube Growth Playbook

## The thesis

Faceless long-form YouTube channels in high-RPM niches, AI-augmented at every production stage, run as a portfolio. Scott Smith's 22-channel operation generates $200K+/month using this exact model. The methodology is replicable. This playbook is the system.

## The 5-step methodology (Scott Smith)

| Step | What | AI Tools | Human Work |
|------|------|----------|------------|
| 1. Niche Selection | Pick high-RPM niche with demand + gap | Claude, Gemini, 1of10, Spotter Studio | Strategic filter |
| 2. Video Ideation | Research channel + topics + validate trends | Claude (4 prompts), Gemini Deep Research, Google Trends | Verify data |
| 3. Script Pack | Build Script Bible → outline → final script | Claude, Gemini, Subscribr (retention QA) | Human script writer (final) |
| 4. Getting First Views | Publish 2/wk, monitor CTR ≥10%, APV ≥30% | AI analytics analysis, TubeBuddy A/B | Thumbnail + title iteration |
| 5. Monetize + Scale | Hit 1K subs + 4K watch hrs → YPP → repeat on channel 2, 3, 4… | Same AI stack | Content team (shared across channels) |

## The economics

- **One channel:** ~$75/video × ~32 videos over 4 months = ~$2,400 spend → $30K+ revenue potential at maturity
- **Portfolio principle:** 80% of videos get <1,000 views. 3 of 22 videos drive 90% of revenue. Normal.
- **RPM matters more than effort:** $12 RPM niche earns 6x what a $2 RPM niche earns for the same view count
- **Long-form only:** Shorts do not pay meaningfully. 10–15 min is the sweet spot.

## The 2026 AI augmentation

Beyond Scott's original stack, this playbook integrates current state-of-the-art:

- **Niche intelligence:** 1of10 (62B-view outlier database) + Spotter Studio (channel-level intel)
- **Script writing:** Claude Opus + Gemini Deep Research + Subscribr retention QA layer
- **Voice:** ElevenLabs v3 (primary) + Cartesia Sonic 3 (drafts) + Chatterbox (open-source overflow)
- **Thumbnails:** 1of10 Thumbnails (niche-trained) + Nano Banana 2 (text rendering) + Photoshop polish
- **B-roll generation:** Veo 3.1 (workhorse) + Kling 3.0 (multi-shot) + Seedance 2.0 (narrative)
- **Assembly:** Pictory 2.0 (auto first cut) + Descript (editor polish)
- **Repurposing:** Clipify (free, local) + Postiz (multi-platform scheduling)

## The quality gates

Every video clears these before publishing:

- **Topic gate:** Validated in Google Trends, no demonetization risk
- **Title gate:** Under 70 chars, primary keyword, emotional hook, 3 variants generated
- **Thumbnail gate:** High contrast, face/focal element, 2–3 variants, niche-winning pattern
- **Script gate:** Hook in first 15s, stakes by 30s mark, research-backed, 10–15 min target
- **Production gate:** Clear VO, matching B-roll, consistent audio, captions, end screen
- **Upload gate:** Title/description/tags/hashtags set, optimal publish time, playlist assigned

## The metrics that matter (Scott Smith's diagnostic)

| CTR | APV | Diagnosis | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| High | High | Winning | Double down — make more in this cluster |
| High | Low | Script problem | Rewrite hook + first 30 seconds |
| Low | High | Packaging problem | New thumbnails + titles |
| Low | Low | Topic mismatch | Rethink: is this topic right for this channel? |
